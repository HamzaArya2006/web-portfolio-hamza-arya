Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me and my development process.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but here are general guidelines: Simple websites (2-4 weeks), Web applications (6-12 weeks), Complex SaaS platforms (3-6 months). I always provide detailed timelines in my proposals.

Do you work with clients outside your timezone?

Absolutely! I work with clients globally and am flexible with communication schedules. I'm available for calls during business hours in most timezones and provide regular updates via email and project management tools.

What's included in your development process?

My process includes: Discovery & planning, Design & prototyping, Development & testing, Deployment & launch, Post-launch support. Each phase includes regular check-ins and milestone reviews.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes! I offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. This ensures your application stays secure and up-to-date.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, and more. I choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and long-term goals.
